Blini with red caviar

Introduction

The modern blini as we know them today are closely associated with Slavic cultures, particularly Russia.

The round form is associated with the sun, the cycle of life, and the unity of family.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Butter or oil for greasing the pan

What to do with this stuff

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add a small amount of butter or oil to coat the surface
  2. Pour a small amount of batter onto the hot skillet, swirling it around to spread thinly and create a round shape

    The size of the blini is up to your preference

  3. Cook for 1-2 minutes or until the edges start to lift and the bottom is golden brown

    Flip the blin and cook the other side for an additional 1-2 minutes until golden

  4. Repeat the process with the remaining batter. Add more butter or oil to the pan as needed
  5. Put inside blin whatever u want

Enjoy
